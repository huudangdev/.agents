# ADR Contract

Use this skill to capture durable engineering decisions.

## Required Inputs

- The decision context and the current change package
- The rejected alternatives
- Verification, rollout, and operational consequences
- Existing ADR conventions in the repo

## Decision Rules

- Capture why, not only what.
- Keep the record small, explicit, and linked from project memory.
- Record consequences and verification impact, even when the decision is uncomfortable.

## Output Contract

- `ADR-[Number]: [Short Name]`
- `Status`
- `Context`
- `Decision`
- `Consequences`
- `Verification Impact`
