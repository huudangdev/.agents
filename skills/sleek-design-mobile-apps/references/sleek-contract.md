# Sleek Mobile Design Contract

Use this skill when the goal is premium-looking mobile polish.

## Required Inputs

- The existing product surface
- Visual language constraints and device assumptions
- Motion, hierarchy, and brand constraints

## Decision Rules

- Keep the design intentional, restrained, and device-aware.
- Do not sacrifice clarity for visual decoration.
- Use motion and contrast only where they improve perception.

## Output Contract

- State the visual direction and key polish moves.
- Identify the interaction details that make it feel premium.
- Describe how the design will be verified on device.
