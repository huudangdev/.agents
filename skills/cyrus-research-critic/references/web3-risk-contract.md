# Web3 Risk Contract

Use this reference when `cyrus-research-critic` needs to produce a skeptical
web3 or wallet review.

## 1. Claim Under Review

- What decentralization claim is being made.
- What problem it is supposed to solve.

## 2. Risk Checks

- Custody and key handling.
- Replay or front-running risk.
- Reentrancy or contract risk.
- RPC or indexer dependency.
- Cost and latency burden.

## 3. Decision

- Use chain.
- Use centralized alternative.
- Defer until threat model is clearer.

## 4. Output

- red flags
- safer alternative
- unresolved questions
