# Brainstorm Output Contract

Use this reference when `product-brainstorming` needs to produce bounded product options or a recommendation memo.

## Required Inputs

- The user pain or business gap.
- The current workaround.
- The reason the workaround is insufficient.

## Output Structure

- Problem statement.
- Options.
- Recommendation.
- Evidence and assumptions.
- Handoff.

## Evidence Rules

- Generate at most three options.
- Include upside, blast radius, and verification burden.
- End with one next step.
