# Antigravity Brainstorm Contract

Use this skill when normal planning is blocked and you need bounded lateral options.

## Required Inputs

- The blocker, constraint, or uncertainty
- Active feature docs and relevant evidence
- The current write scope and verification burden

## Decision Rules

- Generate at most three options.
- Include upside, blast radius, and verification burden for each option.
- Convert the surviving option into spec-ready input.

## Output Contract

- State the blocker and what makes it real.
- Show the bounded options.
- End with concrete next steps that can be routed into planning.
